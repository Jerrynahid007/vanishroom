No Logs

Our philosophy of ephemeral weightlessness dictates that we keep absolute zero records of your communications. Once a room expires or is manually closed, all messages, metadata, and connection traces are permanently destroyed from our volatile memory. We cannot surrender what we do not possess.

lock

Encryption

Every transmission is secured with state-of-the-art end-to-end encryption. The cryptographic keys required to decipher your conversations are generated entirely client-side and exist only within the ephemeral bounds of your current session. The server acts merely as a blind relay, passing encrypted packets without the capability to inspect their contents.

timer

Auto-Expiry

Time is the ultimate sanitizer. Every room is bound by a strict countdown timer. Upon reaching zero, the room and its entire contents are instantly purged. This kinetic expiration process is automatic, irreversible, and requires no user intervention, ensuring your digital footprint vanishes completely when the conversation ends.
